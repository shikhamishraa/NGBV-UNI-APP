LIBRARY

Library

We have a fully digitalized automated Central Library, situated in the main campus, having more than 50,000 books covering all major subject areas having a sitting capacity of more than 100 students at a time. Our reading room has a large range additions of Newspapers, Magazines, Journals and other important write-ups which serve students in a purposeful manner.

 

Besides, central Library there are departmental libraries too, especially for professional subjects. In addition, some departments have Departmental Library facility. A new state of the art library building, having two stories, covering an area of more than 3500 Sq. Mts. approx., built at our Shashi Campus at Jhuthi Tali. This will be synonymous of a modern library equipped with latest gazettes.
